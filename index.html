The College Socialists at The Citadel

Is a shadowy cabal of socialists haunting The Citadel's conservative order? Not to worry, we are only here to help build a New Carolina. We are the student and alumni socialists of The Citadel, the Military College of South Carolina. Interested in joining the fight for political, social, and economic freedom why not join us?

The College Socialists is open to all types of socialists, social democrats, communists, anarchists, & leftists.

Who Is A Bulldog Socialist?

Anyone who fights for justice and equality with a greater world in their hearts, is a Bulldog Socialist. The Bulldog Socialist is an alumni, faculty, and friends association designed to build connections. If it was not for the great support of our Bulldog Socialists, the College Socialists would not be the student club it is today.
